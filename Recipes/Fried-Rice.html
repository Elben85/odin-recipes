Recipe For Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Green Onion
  • 1-2 Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • 1 Bag of Frozen Chopper Veggies
  • 1/2 Bag of Bacon

Instructions

  1. Scramble the eggs
  2. Cook the back and chop into small pieces
  3. Chop the green onion
  4. Defrost bag of frozen veggies
  5. Turn a pan onto medium-high heat and add in a couple spoons of cooking oil
  6. When it starts to heat up, add in some of your chopped green onion
  7. Add the remaining ingredients into the pan and stir fry
  8. Stir fry for approximately 7-8 minutes
  9. Enjot the fried rice by itself, or with added sauces
